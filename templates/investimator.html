POINTS TO REMEMBER

Description:

Investimator is a tool that estimates the price of a stock for five years based on the company’s financial forecast.

Follow the Instructions on how to use for the ease of usage.

  • As of now, Investimator forecast is only available for stocks that are listed for five full financial years.
  • Investimator might show wrong forecast for cyclical stocks (metals, sugar, chemical or textile stocks) because of it’s uncertainty in earnings.
  • The forecast price calculated by the tool may or may not be achieved by the end of a specific year but can be achieved in subsequent years.
  • “Investimator” estimates a stock price based only on data analysis. The forecast price estimated by the tool should not be taken as an Investment advice.
  • Any buy or sell decision based on the output of the tool must not be pursued by an individual or entity without prior consultation with a financial professional.
  • If you had understood the above points, Just get ready and use our tool with ease.
