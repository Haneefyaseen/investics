How to use "Investimator"

  1. Choose the stock you want to forecast from the drop-down menu.
  2. Financial data of previous six years for the stock you’ve chosen will appear.
  3. Scroll down and choose the number of years of forecasting you want.
  4. Click “FORECAST THE FINANCIALS” button. The financial estimate for the number of years you’ve chosen will appear.
  5. Click “FORECAST THE STOCK PRICE” button. The estimated stock price for the next years will appear.
Note:
  • If you are forecasting for a negative PE companies or companies with high uncertainty in profits, It is better to use “Price based on PR”.
  • If you are forecasting for banking or finance stocks, It is better to use “Price based on PB”.
